iPhone Cinema App

The iPhone Cinema App provides users a simple and convenient way to browse theater locations, show times and movie reviews for films currently playing in their area. Users can login through their favorite social media platform, share movie history, invite friends and become a subscriber to earn discounts on tickets and concessions. Working with business owners and developers I was responsible for defining a logical architecture and for creating an appealing visual style and brand identity. This project taught me various ways to effectively integrate social media into applications.
