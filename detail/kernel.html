Kernel Wealth

Kernel had been growing rapidly, expanding its product suite. As a result, retail investors were faced with duplicate functionality spread across three separate areas - each with its own design system and user experience. The challenge was to create a unified, seamless experience that enabled users to set up and manage all recurring investments in one place, regardless of investment type, while respecting the unique constraints of each product. My approach began with understanding user needs, deconstructing the existing product landscape, and identifying core use cases. From there, I rebuilt the experience around a clean, intuitive interface designed to scale effortlessly as the product evolved.
